## Asphalth and Blunite
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:asphalt" title="Asphalt"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:blunite" title="Blunite"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:polished_asphalt" title="Polished Asphalt"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:polished_blunite" title="Polished Blunite"/>

Just like granite, diorite and andesite, asphalt and blunite are two new igneous stone types that generate in the Overworld.
They can also be crafted into polished variants.

## Palms
<Asset width={1024} location="promenade:biome/palm" title="Palm"/>

Palms can generate in desert biomes. They are tall leaping trees that have low-hanging leaves.

### Palm wood set
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:palm_log" title="Palm Log"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:stripped_palm_log" title="Stripped Palm Log"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:palm_planks" title="Palm Planks"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:palm_door" title="Palm Door"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:palm_sign" title="Palm Sign"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:palm_boat" title="Palm Boat"/>

You can cut down a palm tree to get palm logs, which can be crafted into planks. The wood is of a light brown color.

Palm logs feature a unique texture inside the bark, because palms do not have growth rings like other trees.

## Blueberry Bushes
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:blueberry_bush" title="Blueberry Bush"/>
<Asset width={64} height={64} location="promenade:blueberries" title="Blueberries"/>

Blueberry bushes generate in all biomes where sweet berry bushes can generate. They hold blueberries, which can be eaten by players and foxes.
