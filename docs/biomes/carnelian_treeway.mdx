The **carnelian treeway** is a forest biome. It is the only biome where maple trees naturally generate.

## Description
The carnelian treeway reminds of a bright autumn with its blazing red-orange-yellow color palette.

## Generation
It generates in the Overworld as frequently as normal forests.

<Callout variant="info">
    You can tweak with the frequency of biomes in the mod's configuration file.
</Callout>

You can find most of the mobs that spawn in the forest biome in the carnelian treeway.
However, you can also find wolves and foxes.

## Features
### Maple trees

There are 4 variants of maple trees generating in the carnelian treeway:
- <Asset width={48} height={48} location="promenade:sap_maple_sapling"
         title="Sap Maple Sapling"/> **Sap** (Green leaves, depends on the biome)
- <Asset width={48} height={48} location="promenade:vermilion_maple_sapling"
         title="Vermilion Maple Sapling"/> **Vermilion** (Red leaves)
- <Asset width={48} height={48} location="promenade:fulvous_maple_sapling"
         title="Fulvous Maple Sapling"/> **Fulvous** (Orange leaves)
- <Asset width={48} height={48} location="promenade:mikado_maple_sapling"
         title="Mikado Maple Sapling"/> **Mikado** (Yellow leaves)

Each tree feature different saplings and leaves, but the wood is the same.


When you strip a natural maple log, you have a 10% chance to find syrup drooling on its side, which you can collect with a glass bottle: <Asset width={32} height={32} location="promenade:maple_syrup_bottle" title="Maple Syrup Bottle" />
